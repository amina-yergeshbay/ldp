Transformational Leadership,

Building on Bass’s (1985) research, the focus is on motivating followers through a captivating vision, intellectual engagement, and personalized attention. In our charity event simulation, my team and I incorrectly labeled our actions as transformational, believing we were enacting significant changes. In truth, we had leveraged Transactional Leadership: we assigned specific tasks, provided incentives for their completion, and concentrated on performance measures. This incorrect application highlighted my lack of understanding of the essential traits of transformational leaders - particularly the capacity to convey a vision that extends beyond immediate objectives and to nurture intrinsic motivation in team members. The power of Transformational Leadership lies in its ability to resonate with the values of followers and promote creativity; however, critics note that it can be perceived as manipulative if the leader’s vision is primarily self-serving and devoid of ethical principles (Tourish, 2013).
